Non-fungible Tokens

  • Recent controversial major trend.
  • Built on the Ethereum blockchain, so huge energy wastage.
  • Allows some degree of ‘uniqueness’ in software.

Web3

  • Touted as ‘the future of the internet’.
  • A decentralised, blockchain-based internet.
  • Will take immense amounts of energy, but can ensure privacy.
  • Freedom from governmental control, especially in authoritarian regimes.

Evaluation

Pros:

  • Topical - Cryptocurrencies are huge lately. NFTs have just taken off, and Web3 can take place in the near future.
  • Influential - he Blockchain is considered to be the ‘future of worldwide privacy’ and could become the global privacy standard.
  • Personally interesting - Blockchain’s simple nature yet underlying complexity offers a strong and inviting challenge.

Cons

  • Overdone - The Blockchain is a majorly studied part of technology, especially from a non-technical perspective; there is very little new area that I can explore.
  • Unstable - Cryptocurrencies, and the Blockchain in general are very volatile, requiring huge amounts of energy input from node computers.
  • Low future prospects - Due to energy requirements, countries are beginning to outlaw technologies relating to the Blockchain, like Cryptocurrency mining.
